About the NGO

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization based in Chhattisgarh. Founded on September 23, 2020, the foundation works across India to support underprivileged communities through sustainable and inclusive initiatives.

Our Ongoing Projects

Project SEVA

Providing food, clothing, and essential support to underprivileged people.

Project BACHPANSHALA

Supporting children with education, digital literacy, and life skills.

Project JEEV

Helping stray animals through feeding, rescue, and protection initiatives.

Project UDAAN

Empowering women through skill development and financial independence.

Project PRAKRITI

Promoting tree plantation, sustainability, and environmental awareness.

Project VIKAS

Improving employability through internships, training, and skill development.

Social Impact

InAmigos Foundation aims to create a more compassionate and empowered society. Through its initiatives, it supports education, hunger relief, women empowerment, animal welfare, environment protection, and youth skill development.
